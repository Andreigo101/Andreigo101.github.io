Fun Facts About T. rex

  • T. rex had banana-shaped teeth up to 30 cm long.
  • It had one of the most powerful bite forces ever recorded.
  • Its sense of smell was better than most modern animals.

More T. rex Facts

  • Ran up to 20 km/h at full speed.
  • Only had two fingers per hand.
  • Lived 68-66 million years ago.

T-Rex Tail

  • The tail helped balance the massive body during movement.
  • It acted as a counterweight to the heavy head.
  • The tail contributed to quick turning while hunting prey.

T-Rex Body

  • Massive rib cage housed powerful lungs and vital organs.
  • Muscular legs supported its large frame and enabled running.
  • Its body was covered in scales, possibly with some proto-feathers.

T-Rex Head

  • Strongest bite force of any land animal ever recorded.
  • Excellent vision with forward-facing eyes for depth perception.
  • Large olfactory bulbs allowed an exceptional sense of smell.
